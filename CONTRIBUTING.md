Thank you for contributing! Here are just some rules you must follow while doing your thing.

General conduct:
  1. Don't make any comments that are innappropriate.
  2. Be respectful while talking to other contributors.
Coding conduct:
  1. Keep the general coding style intact, its how the library is so small in the first place.
  2. You can add your own variable names and naming conventions, but make sure they stay on topic and aren't random.
  3. Make sure the code is cross-compatible to your best ability, please.
Reporting bugs:
  1. Start an issue with a clear description of the issue.
  2. Make sure the bug you are facing occurs consistently and does not occur due to your code.
  3. Clarify your work environment, as in your OS, IDE, etc. to further assist in the resolution of the bug.
Feature requests:
  1. Open an issue described "Feature Recommendation", "Feature Request", or something similar, describing your feature request.
  2. Make sure your feature is relevant to the library.
Licensing
  1. By contributing, you agree that your contributions will be licensed under the project’s existing license.
